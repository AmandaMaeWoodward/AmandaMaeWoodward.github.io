Amanda Mae Woodward

I am a PhD student in the Lab for Early Social Cognition at the University of Maryland, College Park and a Lecturer at Loyola University Maryland. I enjoy teaching statistics and research methods to undergraduate and graduate students.My research focuses on how children use observed social interactions to predict future behavior and evaluate others.

Before starting my PhD program, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Biology and Psychology from Loyola University Maryland in 2014.
